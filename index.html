Allison Yang, MD

Allison Yang, MD is a gastroenterology/hepatology fellow at Brigham and Women’s Hospital, a teaching affiliate of Harvard Medical School, in Boston, MA.

She has clinical interests in pancreatic diseases, including acute and chronic pancreatitis and pancreatic cysts. Her research focuses on the post-discharge management of acute pancreatitis and early readmissions, and the use of mobile health applications in clinical care.
